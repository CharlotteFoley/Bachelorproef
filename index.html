" If you knew
how much I love you,
you'd cry for joy "

by Charlotte Foley

Living with two people that are merged into one.
You feel like you are losing one of the two.
You feel them slipping away from you.

You are grieving for someone who is still alive.

Every moment when they are normal, themselves;

you forget,

you forgive,

and you pretend.

You press down your feelings,
you put them away,
and try to forget about them.

To save the moment of normality

just for a while.

To keep the peace.

But hope kills.

It makes you lose a part of yourself.

You'll always be prepared

to be let down,

to be disappointed,

to be second best,

to be never good enough,

to not be listened to,

to be ignored,

to be shouted at,

to not feel important,

to be fooled,

to be mislead,

to be manipulated,

to be heartbroken,

to be walked away from,

ran away from,

walked out on,

to feel alone.
